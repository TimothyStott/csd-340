Career Highlights

Wanting to fly from a young age, Kyle used his military career to start makign that a reality. Kyle spent most of his career stationed on the East Coast. He spent some time in training and then began working as a crew chief on the UH-1Y platform in New River, NC. During his career he completed multiple deployments and saw many different countries. One of his greatest accomplishments was to become a Weapons and Tactics Instructor by completeing the WTI course in Yuma, Arizona. His earlier career allowed him to gain a large amount of knowledge for the H-1 platform and he ended his career by serving as the H-1 Assistant Program Manager in Cherry Point, NC.
