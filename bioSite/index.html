The Home

Kyle's grandparents have lived in Eau Claire since the mid 60' where they run a local business. In the early 70's they bought the farm as to use as hobby farm and they have lived there ever since. Ever since he can remember Kyle has considered this home.

The School

Kyle attends Southern Utah University, in Cedar City Utah. He has finished one year of the 3 year program and he plans on finishing with his private pilot's license including the instrumental rating, his commerical license, and his certified flight instructor license. He doesn't mind exactly where he ends up flying, but he is currently interested in Fedex, UPS, and Delta Airlines.

The Jeep

Kyle is restoring a 1953 Willys CJ that he received from his grandfather. The Jeep was a farm vehicle used for the last 50 years on his grandparents farm. The past two years have seen a lot of restoration.
